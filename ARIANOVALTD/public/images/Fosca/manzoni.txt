Tasting Notes
Rich wine on the nose, with scents ranging from floral, such as linden and acacia, to fruity, with notes of banana, peach and tropical fruit. On the palate, the wine’s good structure is perfectly integrated with acidity, making the sip long and persistent.
Pairings
Ideal for fish soups, white meats, green asparagus and herb risottos.
Tenutefosca Monovarietali
Harvest Period
Grapes harvested in mid-September in the Sarcedo vineyard.
Vinification
Grapes are softly pressed and vinified in stainless steel at controlled temperature. The wine is then racked into stainless steel to preserve as much freshness and typical varietal notes as possible. After 4 months it is finally bottled.
