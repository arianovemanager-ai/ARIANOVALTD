Tasting Notes
Rose petal color, very fine bubble that caresses the palate. Savory and creamy, floral, its freshness gives it drinkability and digestibility.
Pairings
Silvia’s bubbles are ideal not only for a good aperitif, but also as an accompaniment to seafood crudités appetizers, fresh cheeses, and fish entrees.
Silvia Tenutefosca bottiglia
Harvest Period
Grapes harvested in late August.
Vinification
Soft pressing of grapes and fermentation at a controlled temperature of 16°C for 2 weeks. The wine then remains in contact with its fine lees for 4 months, during which frequent batonnage is performed so as to extract all the sweetness of the yeasts. Then re-fermentation takes place in an autoclave (Charmat Method), where it remains for another 7 months before being bottled.
